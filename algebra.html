Algebra Course

Course Description

This algebra course is designed for beginners who are looking to improve their understanding of fundamental algebra concepts. The course includes 3 units with 10 skills per unit to help you build a strong foundation in algebra. You will learn how to solve linear equations, graph quadratic equations, and factor polynomials.

Course Info

Number of Units: 3

Number of Skills: 30

Time to Complete: 10 weeks

Difficulty Level: Beginner

Curriculum

Unit 1 - Linear Equations

Solve one-step linear equations
Solve two-step linear equations
Solve multi-step linear equations
Solve equations with variables on both sides
Solve literal equations
Solve word problems involving linear equations
Graph linear equations in two variables
Find slope and y-intercept from an equation
Find the equation of a line given slope and y-intercept
Find the equation of a line given two points

Unit 2 - Quadratic Equations

Solve quadratic equations by factoring
Solve quadratic equations by completing the square
Solve quadratic equations by using the quadratic formula
Solve word problems involving quadratic equations
Graph quadratic equations in two variables
Find the vertex and axis of symmetry of a quadratic equation
Find the maximum or minimum value of a quadratic equation
Solve problems involving maximum or minimum value of a quadratic equation
Use quadratic equations to model real-world situations
Understand the discriminant and its significance

Unit 3 - Polynomials

Identify monomials, binomials, and polynomials
Add and subtract polynomials
Multiply polynomials using FOIL method
Multiply polynomials using distributive method
Factor trinomials with leading coefficient 1
Factor trinomials with leading coefficient not equal to 1
Factor perfect square trinomials
Factor the difference of two squares
Solve word problems involving polynomials
Understand the relationship between roots and factoring
